Welcome to the prologue

With short chainstays, medium reach, high bottom bracket and a slack headtube.

It is the perfect bike for narrow steep or flat quick turning trails with lots of roots and rocks

It can support 29" tires as wide as 3" in the rear while using a regular BSA bottom bracket.

This makes it perfect for use in sand and snow when the seasons change.

What really makes us love this frame though is its rigity. The rear seatstays pass the

seat tube and attach to the top tube and the seat tube making for less flex left to right!
